About This Destination

The capital city of India, Delhi is a vibrant metropolis that seamlessly blends the old and new. From the historic Red Fort and Jama Masjid to the modern India Gate and Lotus Temple, Delhi offers a fascinating mix of Mughal architecture, British colonial heritage, and contemporary urban development.

Alleppey (Alappuzha), known as the "Venice of the East," is the heart of Kerala's backwater region. Famous for its intricate network of canals, coir industry, Chinese fishing nets, and traditional houseboat cruises.

Immerse yourself in the rich culture, history, and traditions of this incredible destination. From ancient monuments to modern marvels, every moment promises to be unforgettable.
